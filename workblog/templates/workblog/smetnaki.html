Properties


  • Finished in less than a week
  • Heavy use of Figma for design
  • Integration of internationalization and localization, so everyone can understand
  • Easy to add upcoming concerts


Story

Smetnaki band had presence only on social networks but it didn't have its own website.

When saxophone player's father made a cool design for a concert poster, Rok was inspired by it and created the website.

Rok started with designing the UI of the website in Figma. That is also how he spent the majority of the time on the project.

Previously he had already made a project in Django, where he had to use localization and internationalization so the Smetnaki was a nice example where this knowledge was proven usable.
